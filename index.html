03 — Research & Experience

Academic journey

  1. 2023 — present

    PhD Researcher — Autonomous Systems Lab

    Stanford University · Stanford, CA

    Developing provably-safe motion planners for legged robots operating in unstructured outdoor environments. Work combines barrier function theory with learned terrain models.

    • "Certified Safe Navigation via Neural Barrier Functions" — ICRA 2025 (submitted)
  2. Summer 2023

    Research Intern — Robotics Division

    Google DeepMind · Mountain View, CA

    Worked on sim-to-real transfer for manipulation policies using domain-randomised rendering. Reduced the sim-to-real gap by 35% on a benchmark grasping suite.

  3. 2022 — 2023

    Undergraduate Researcher

    IIT Bombay · Systems & Control Lab

    Bachelor's thesis on LiDAR-inertial odometry for GPS-denied environments. Implemented a tightly-coupled EKF on resource-constrained embedded hardware.

    • "Lightweight LiDAR-IMU Fusion for UAV State Estimation" — IROS 2023
  4. Summer 2022

    Software Engineering Intern

    Samsung R&D Institute · Bangalore, India

    Optimised on-device inference for a face anti-spoofing model, achieving 3× speedup through quantisation and operator fusion on the Exynos NPU.
