03 — Research & Experience

Academic journey

  1. September 2024 — Present

    Student Researcher — Nano-Photonic Devices

    Pandit Deendayal Energy University · Ahmedabad, India

    Ongoing Student Research Project (SRP) on computational inverse design of nano-photonic sensors for colorimetric fluid detection. Using machine learning to optimise device geometry, replacing costly forward simulation loops.

    • Poster presented at Indo Quantum Summit 2026
  2. November 2025 — Present

    Independent Project — P-bits & Probabilistic Logic

    Self-Directed · PDEU

    Developing a Python-based probabilistic solver for the Max-Cut Problem using p-bits (probabilistic bits). Targeting hard combinatorial optimisation problems with stochastic computing approaches inspired by neuromorphic hardware.

  3. July 2025 — Present

    Treasurer — IEEE CS Student Branch Chapter

    IEEE · Pandit Deendayal Energy University

    Office bearer collaborating with 45+ members to organise expert lectures, student-led sessions, and technical workshops on Computer Science and Engineering topics across the university community.

  4. 2023 — Present

    B.Tech in ECE · Minor in Data Science

    Pandit Deendayal Energy University · Ahmedabad

    Relevant coursework: Analog Electronics, Circuits & Systems, Digital Logic Design, Digital Signal Processing, Linear Algebra, Analog Communication. Recipient of the Reliance Foundation Undergraduate Scholarship. 5th Place, MATLAB Innovation Challenge.
